The interior region of the polygon is called the face. When many faces are connected together they create a network of faces called a polygon mesh (also referred to as a polyset or a polygonal object). #### Polygon component **Vertices, edges, and faces** are the basic components of polygons. When you model with polygons you usually use three-sided polygons called triangles or four-sided polygons called quadrilaterals (quads). Maya also supports the creation of polygons with more than four sides (n-gons) but they are not as commonly used for modeling. ![poly01](img/maya/polygon/poly.png) ![poly02](img/maya/polygon/edge.png) ![poly03](img/maya/polygon/mn.png) #### Non-manifold, 2-manifold ![poly04](img/maya/polygon/manyf.png) #### Normal ![nol01](img/maya/polygon/nor01.png) - Face normal ![nol01](img/maya/polygon/fn.png) - Vertex normal ![nol01](img/maya/polygon/vn.png) #### Smooth method ![nol01](img/maya/polygon/smthmethod.png) #### Shading ![shd01](img/maya/polygon/sha.png) #### Texturing ![tex01](img/maya/polygon/texturing.png) #### Boolean ![boo01](img/maya/polygon/bl01.png) ![boo01](img/maya/polygon/bl02.png) Making: - NURBs: Surfaces/Edit NURBS/Booleans 1.
