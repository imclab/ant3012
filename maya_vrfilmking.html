# Virtual Filmmaking Mastering Autodesk Maya 2013 Chapter 2 Help file [Maya 2014 online Help](http://download.autodesk.com/global/docs/maya2014/en_us/), [Maya tutorial files](http://usa.autodesk.com/adsk/servlet/index?siteID=123112&id=9502844&linkID=9242256) ### The virtual cameras in Maya. >*As with all filmmaking, the story is told through the camera.* Although the technical aspects of using the cameras are not difficult to learn, mastering the art of virtual cinematography can take years of practice. ### Depth of Field (DoF) rendering #### Scene preparation 1. Create three plane and five balls on the Cylinder (Create/Polygon Primitives & Edit/Duplicate Special) and align them. 2. Create a camera with a aim (Create/Cameras/Camera and Aim) and check it at the Outliner (two plane mode -> at the left panel or panels/Layouts/Two Panes Side by Side). 3. Choose the camera and transform Snapping the Aim to the main focus point of the object). 4. Adjust the position of camera (panels/Look through Selected) - Choosing Camera properties: (select Camera -> Attribute Editor) - Angle of View, Near and Far clip, Film Gate ![sp03](img/maya/shading/camera.png) 5. Measure the distance between main object to the camera (Create/Measure Tools/Distance Tool) 6. Use the distance for the Focus Distance in the Attribute Editor of the camera - Check Depth of Field (select Camera -> Attribute Editor), change the Focus Distance and F Stop ![sp04](img/maya/shading/dof.png) 7. Render the scene. Do the basic render settings. - Choosing Rendering properties: (Window/Rendering Editors/Render Settings) - Work on Common settings - File name, Image format, Choose camera, Choose Image Size ![sp01](img/maya/shading/render_common.png) - Work on Specific settings -> Maya Software - Quality, Anti-aliasing, Raytracing, Motion Blur ![sp02](img/maya/shading/render_maya.png) #### Use depth of field and motion blur ![dof01](img/maya/shading/dof_render.png) #### Inspirational source: [Limbo](http://limbogame.org/media/) #### Review: [Tumblr](http://2014ant3012.tumblr.com/) ___________________________
